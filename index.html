The Story of Amayara and Kiara

Once upon a time, in the magical valley of Lunaria, lived two sisters — Amayara and Kiara.

Amayara was calm and wise. She understood the whispers of the wind and the songs of the birds.

Kiara was adventurous and full of spark. Her laughter could make flowers bloom.

One night, a violet light appeared in the sky. The next morning, they discovered a glowing crystal.

Together, they climbed the tallest mountain and unlocked an ancient gateway of stars.

From that day on, Amayara and Kiara became the protectors of Lunaria — guiding others with wisdom and courage.
